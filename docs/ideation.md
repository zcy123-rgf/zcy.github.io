# 3. Ideation & Alternatives

- Location-based story points with AR overlays
- Different navigation styles (map view / list view)
- Gamified fragment collection mechanics
- AI chatbot interface designs

## 3.2 Design Alternatives Comparison
We compared three distinct design concepts and selected the one that best met our goals:

### Concept A: Static Story Guide
- **Pros**: Simple to build, low technical complexity.
- **Cons**: Passive experience, no gamification, low engagement.

### Concept B: AR-only Experience
- **Pros**: Strong immersion through AR overlays.
- **Cons**: No AI storytelling, no clear progression path, relies heavily on device performance.

### Concept C: Playful AR + AI + Fragment Collection (Final Design)
- **Pros**:
  1. Combines location-based AR, AI storytelling, and gamified collection.
  2. Supports both Quick Explore and Deep Explore modes.
  3. High engagement with clear progression and rewards.
- **Cons**: Requires more development effort for integration.

We chose **Concept C** because it best addresses our identified user needs, balances immersion with playfulness, and is scalable for future extensions.
